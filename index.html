As a Chartered Accountant with a specialisation in capital markets, I work at the intersection where ambitious companies meet institutional-grade capital. My practice is built on the belief that access to the right capital — at the right structure, at the right time — is a fundamental growth enabler.

Over three years of focused execution across PE/VC fundraises, SME and Main Board IPOs, platform migrations, and FPOs, I have developed a methodology that treats every transaction as unique — because it is. Cookie-cutter approaches don't serve founder ambitions or investor mandates.

The FinGuide platform extends this commitment beyond transactions — making high-quality financial guidance accessible to a broader audience, demystifying capital markets for the next generation of business builders.

Whether you're a growth-stage company looking for your first institutional round, or a listed SME eyeing the Main Board — I bring the network, the structure, and the conviction to get you there.
